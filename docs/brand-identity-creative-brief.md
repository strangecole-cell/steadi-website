# Steadi Brand Identity Creative Brief

## 1. Brand Background

**Brand Name & Category:** Steadi is a women's life-stage supplement brand designed to support hormonal balance, mood, and overall wellness during menopause and perimenopause.

**Brand Origin Story:** Our team has spent years building products across different health and wellness categories, always noticing the same gap: a lack of intelligent, modern solutions for women in perimenopause and menopause. One of our founders, a Physician Assistant, has witnessed countless patients navigating the changes in their bodies and minds with few effective supplements available. Steadi was born from these frustrations. We're serious about science and focused on creating products that genuinely help women feel steady again, not just another clinical or dated quick fix.

**Current Situation:** Pre-launch stage. Formulation is being finalized for our base menopause support (capsule form); line extensions in exploration (perimenopause, sleep, mood, cognition). Amazon US is the primary launch channel.

**Vision & Ambition:** To become the most trusted and modern menopause brand on Amazon and beyond — known for clinically credible, emotionally intelligent formulations that evolve with women through life stages.

### Brand Feel / Emotional World

Steadi should feel like the calm that follows a deep breath or deep sigh of relief. It quiets the noise, the racing thoughts, the restless body, the emotional static. It gives her a sense of inner steadiness and space to face what comes next with clarity and composure.

**Brand Mood Summary in Five Words:** Steady. Modern. Assured. Empathetic. Clear.

---

## 2. Brand Purpose & Belief System

**Purpose:** To help women feel steady — physically, emotionally, and mentally — as they navigate hormonal transitions.

**Values:**
- Science-backed
- Emotionally attuned to women's needs
- Respect for women's intelligence

**Belief:** Women deserve products that provide real relief during hormonal changes in their life, not confusion, clutter, or clichés.

### Consumer Tension

Menopause isn't the sole point of tension; it's the chaos around it.

Everywhere she looks, there are products claiming relief, influencers promising balance, and doctors offering platitudes. Yet nothing truly speaks to her. Her body feels foreign, her thoughts slip away, she's restless and hot at night and heavy with fatigue by day. These changes are not just about her body, it's also about her mind. She's overwhelmed by choices but underwhelmed by trust. Her life can't stop just because she's feeling like this. What she wants most is calm, something real, something to steady herself and quiet all the noise so she can get back to her life.

And then there's perimenopause — even more obscure, more isolating. She's "too young" for menopause, she tells herself, while juggling a career, a partner, and little kids who still need her energy. Yet something feels off: her cycles are irregular, her mood unpredictable, her sleep fractured, her body unfamiliar. She wonders if it's stress, hormones, or just her losing her edge. No one's talking about it, and the silence makes her feel even more alone.

---

## 3. Consumer Target & Insight

**Primary Target:** Women aged 40–60, entering perimenopause or menopause. Educated, health-aware, digitally savvy, and comfortable researching ingredients online (Amazon, Reddit, TikTok).

**Consumer Insight:** She is tired of not recognizing herself. Her body feels unpredictable, her sleep is inconsistent, her mind foggy, and her patience thin. Every headline, influencer, and supplement promises a fix, but nothing matches her current lived experience. She is not searching for miracles; she is searching for remedies she can trust. She wants clear information and proven ingredients that respect her intelligence. More than anything, she wants to feel centered again, grounded in her own skin while her body and mind keep shifting.

**Emotional Need:** Feeling centered and grounded when her body and mind are in motion.

**Functional Need:** A supplement that measurably supports hormonal, cognitive, and emotional steadiness (metabolism, energy, mood, and sleep).

---

## 4. Brand Positioning

**Brand Promise:** Science-backed formulations designed to help women feel steady through hormonal transitions.

**Reason to Believe:** Evidence-based and quality products formulated by scientists and a medical professional.

**Emotional Benefit:** Feeling centered and grounded when everything with her body and mind is in motion.

**Brand Idea (Essence):** Steadi yourself when everything is changing.

---

## 5. Personality & Voice

**Personality Traits:** Calm, intelligent, composed, empathetic, quietly confident.

**Voice & Tone:**
- Gentle authority; speaks from evidence, not ego
- Minimalist language; trusts the reader's intelligence
- Avoid clichés (e.g. "Embrace the change!", "Hot flash, more like hot mess!", "Take back control!")
- Supportive but not overly sweet or sensitive — straightforward

**Tagline / Slogan Options:**
- Steadi. Set. Go.
- Stay Steadi.
- Steadi mind. Steadi body. Steadi you.

---

## 6. Distinctive Brand Assets

**Foundational Assets:**
- **Logo:** Completed and filed with the USPTO
- **Color palette:** Soft neutrals and desaturated tones
- **Typography:** Modern, serious but not clinical
- **Fonts:** Piazzolla (serif) + Zalando Sans (sans-serif)

**First Product Label:** First launch will be a capsule in a white bottle aimed towards menopause daily support. Unique positioning: metabolism, GLP-1 activation, weight control. Also provides baseline menopause relief (hot flashes, mood support, hormone balance, sleep support).

---

## 7. Design Guardrails

- **Typography Rules:** Modern sans-serif, variable weight for calm emphasis
- **Imagery Guidelines:** Natural light, authentic moments of stillness and calm
- **Iconography System:** Line-based, rounded corners, symmetrical shapes
- **Motion / Digital Expression:** Smooth transitions, "ease-in" animations

## 9. Inspiration & Competitive Landscape

**Comparable/Competitive Brands:** Estroven (main competitor), Amberen, MENO, Bonafide

**Differentiation:** Unlike Estroven and Amberen, Steadi bridges science-backed with emotional intelligence, speaking to women who research as much as they feel. Appealing to emotions rather than being clinical in tone and appearance. Serious, but never clinical.

**Visual Territories:**
- The Modern Woman: confident simplicity, not pastel, feminine clichés
- Scientific: evidence-based clarity, balanced compositions, serious but not clinical

**Do's:**
- Calm color fields
- Elegant typography
- Modern product photography

**Don'ts:**
- Exclusive or clichéd pink/purple palettes
- Medicalized iconography
- Overused "balance" symbols like yin-yangs or scales

---

## 10. Brand Architecture & Commercial Framework

Steadi operates as a **unified masterbrand** supported by clear, scalable product architecture:

- **Two life-stage base formulas:** Perimenopause + Menopause (foundational daily support)
- **Cross-stage targeted relief range** organized by need state: Sleep, Mood, Vaginal Dryness, Immunity, and future extensions
- **Multiple delivery formats:** Capsules, gummies
- Visual identity maintains cohesion while allowing clear product differentiation through colors, textures, and shapes
